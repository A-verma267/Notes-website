Frequently Asked Questions

Is this course suitable for complete beginners?

+

Absolutely! This course is designed for complete beginners with no prior programming experience. We start from the very basics and gradually build up to advanced concepts.

What software do I need to install?

+

You'll need a C compiler like GCC and a text editor or IDE. We provide detailed setup instructions for Windows, Mac, and Linux. Popular choices include Code::Blocks, Dev-C++, or Visual Studio Code.

How long does it take to complete the course?

+

The course contains approximately 20 hours of content. If you study 1-2 hours per day, you can complete it in 2-4 weeks. However, you can learn at your own pace.

Do I get a certificate upon completion?

+

Yes! Upon successful completion of all modules and projects, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can add to your resume and LinkedIn profile.

Is there any support available if I get stuck?

+

Yes! We have an active community forum and WhatsApp group where you can ask questions and get help from instructors and fellow students. We're committed to helping you succeed.
